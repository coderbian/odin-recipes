Vietnamese Shaking Beef

Home

Description

One of the most famous traditional food in VietNam. The flavor is excellent, all people travelling in Viet Nam must try it once in life

Some famous restaurants: Hoc & Thao traditional meals, Huong Que restaurant, ...

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Yellow onion
  • Soy sauce
  • And so on ...

Steps

  1. Cut your steak into 1 inch cubes and marinate with oyster sauce, soy sauce, salt, pepper, minced garlic, sugar and msg
  2. Cut your yellow onion into half moons, and cut your green onion into 1-2 inch segments
  3. Add oil to a ripping hot pan and sear your beef. Let the beef sit for 10-20 seconds before shaking or stirring and repeat. You want the beef to cook for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the sliced onions to the beef and continue to shake/stir until the onions are barely tender, about 2 more minutes. Turn off the heat, add the green onions and stir through to wilt.
  5. Serve the beef over a bed of greens and vegetables of your choice.
